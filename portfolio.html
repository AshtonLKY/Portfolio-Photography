About

The Eye
Behind the Lens

I'm Lena Voss — a photographer and visual director based in Berlin. With over eight years behind the lens, I specialize in editorial, fashion, and commercial work that pushes aesthetic boundaries.

My work has appeared in VOGUE Germany, AnOther Magazine, and i-D. I collaborate closely with brands and individuals to construct images that don't just document — they resonate.
